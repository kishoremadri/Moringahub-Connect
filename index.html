Quality & Standards
Quality is non-negotiable. It protects both buyers and suppliers.

What We Look For

  • Shade-dried leaves wherever possible.
  • Fine, green powder ‚Äì not dull or brown.
  • Moisture levels generally at or below 7‚Äì8%.
  • Clean processing environment with minimal contamination risk.
  • Food-grade sacks or bags for transport and storage.

Documentation & Testing

  • Basic supplier details and declarations.
  • Lab test reports where available, especially for larger orders.
  • Photos or short videos of processing and packing if needed.
  • Optional: pre-shipment sample approval for bulk buyers.
