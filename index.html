For Suppliers – Grow Your Sales
Turn your moringa capacity into repeat, higher-value orders.

Why Partner With Us?

  • Get access to buyers you may not reach directly.
  • Guidance on what buyers expect in quality and documentation.
  • Start with trial orders, then grow into repeat contracts.
  • Less time spent searching for buyers, more time producing.

Supplier Requirements

  • Consistent supply capacity and honest communication.
  • Hygienic processing, preferably shade drying.
  • Ability to maintain agreed quality and packing.
  • Willingness to share basic documents and, where possible, lab reports.
Quality & Standards
Quality is non‑negotiable. It protects both buyers and suppliers.

What We Look For

  • Shade‑dried leaves wherever possible.
  • Fine, green powder – not dull or brown.
  • Moisture levels generally at or below 7–8%.
  • Clean processing environment with minimal contamination risk.
  • Food‑grade sacks or bags for transport and storage.

Documentation & Testing

  • Basic supplier details and declarations.
  • Lab test reports where available, especially for larger orders.
  • Photos or short videos of processing and packing if needed.
  • Optional: pre‑shipment sample approval for bulk buyers.
